Connect to Greenwich Students


Which Programme representative you want to get in touch?

What to ask from our Programme Representative?

  • Any Programme related information?
  • How the Programme is being accessed(Lectures/Labs)?
  • What are the career opportutnities after this Programme?
  • How's Student Life at University of Greenwich?
  • Any sort of Accomodation Advice?

What NOT to ask from our Programme Representative?

  • What is the Admission Process?
  • What are the entry Requirements?
  • Any sort of Admission related queries(CAS/Visas/Fees/Scholarships)
  • When is the programme intake?
  • Any sort of Personal details(Mobile No./What's App)

Note: You can email us at international@gre.ac.uk and we will help you with your journey to Greenwich.
