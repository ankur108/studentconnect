Why Study at University of Greenwich?

Why choose Greenwich?

Located at the Heart of the UK

Our Greenwich Campus is located just 10 mins from Central London while our Avery Hill is 30 mins from Central London.Our Medway Campus is based in Kent which is great to explore the green rolling hills, wooded valleys and far-reaching landscapes.There is a shuttle bus service which takes 40 mins from our Medway Campus to Greenwich Campus.

Located at the center, it gives students an opportunity to explore different culture and traditions, meet thier potential employers,attend great events & seminars and make connections for the future.

Most Beautiful Campus in London

Our Greenwich Campus has been listed in the Top 10 Most Beautiful Campus in the UK and #1 in London. You will definately get a feel of studying in a UK University along with great attractions in Greenwich town like The O2, Greenwich Park, Maritime Museums, Royal Observatory Greenwich, and much more. You can watch a Vlog from one of our students on Youtube on greats things to do in Greenwich.

Great Learning Environment

University has a great learning environment offering both practical and theoretical experience of your chosen programme. Along with course lectures and labs, you will also be asigned with a personal tutor who will assist you with your studies and student life.

Our Stockwell Street Library is a hub for students who wish to expand thier knowledge beyond lectures. If you haven't got a laptop, Don't worry! Library has a Laptop Loan service which gives you a laptop as long as you need it within the Library. Our all three Campus has a free Wifi access which gives you on-demand internet access.

Greenwich's Student Union

Student Life won't complete without any fun & activities. Greenwich's Students Union is the heart of Student Life. We have more than 50 Societies and 17 Sports Societies which students can join during thier time at Greenwich.

Our Student Union also run its own Clubs & Bars based at Greenwich & Medway Campus with amazing pool,table football games etc. If you are looking for a fun time with your friends, SU is great place to hang out. Find out more about Greenwich's Students Union here.

On Campus Student Jobs

London can be quite expensive for International Students and also being in the list of expensive cities in the World. The University offer wide range of Student Jobs to students during the term-time and non-term time of the year. You can look for our Student Jobs here.

University also has a wonderful Student Ambassador Scheme which offers Zero hour job contract role during the year according to your avaliablity. You can know more about the scheme here.

International Students

The University welcomes applications from international and EU students. Approximately 30% of students in Greenwich are International Students,so you won't be the first one.The University is itself International as Student from more than 140 countries comes every year to study. You will have the opportunity to meet people and make true friends from all continents, and that is just amazing.

Our team of International Student Advisers is here to help and advise on immigration issues and personal & welfare matters impacting on your studies. A free and confidential service is provided to all University of Greenwich international students.

Many students are concerned about being socially excluded because of their origin or level of English. However, this is not the case at the University of Greenwich. London is a very multicultural city, and Greenwich is the clearest example to see how friendship and tolerance are fully respected. I have never experienced any kind of racist/discriminatory experience, and I am sure Greenwich staff would strongly respond against and would condemn any kind of similar conduct.

Erasmus+ Scheme

The University of Greenwich has participated in Study Abroad and Erasmus+ programmes for many years enabling incoming and outgoing students and staff to benefit from experiencing new countries and cultures.Students can choose from a wide range of host institutions in EU countries, such as the Netherlands, Poland, Spain, Italy, among others.

All students who are accepted for Erasmus+ are considered for an Erasmus+ Grant, which is a contribution towards living costs whilst studying or working abroad. The grant is based on monthly amounts, which vary from country to country.

" In my case, I was chosen to study in the North of France during my second academic year. The Erasmus+ programme has been the best experience at university for me. I improved my French skills exponentially, made friends from France and from all over the world, got the chance to travel around Europe, explore some of the unknown Paris neighbours, and got used to French food.My Erasmus coordinator was always there to help me: I struggled a bit choosing modules and for credit conversion. He contacted the French university where I studied, and sorted it out! My grade conversion was in the end well done! "
-Abel Martinez, BA Hons Languages and International Relations

Social Enterprise Challenge

Got any Business Idea?
Greenwich can make it real with its excellent Entrepreneurship Challenges

The University of Greenwich hosts Social Enterprise and Enterprise Challenge every year for Greenwich Students only giving them a platform to pitch thier Business Idea and win a start-up investment of £1k-£3k and £5k repectively. It Happens every year, so even if you loose, you can still take part next year.

" I applied for the Social Enterprise Challenge. I partnered up with one of my classmates to work on a Social Enterprise. On passing stage 1, we were provided with a mentor to work with us on our business plan. Our mentor provided insightful observations on how we could go about devising a strategy and implementing our Social Enterprise. We met our mentor regularly based on our availability and prepared for the finals. During the finals, we had 10 mins to explain our business idea to a panel of judges and had 1 minute to pitch our idea to everyone present for the final event. However, we didn’t win any prizes but we gained a lot of valuable knowledge with the help of our mentor. We will apply again for Social Enterprise this year! "
-Aadil Hushye,International Business,BA Hons
