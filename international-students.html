Frequently Asked Questions


I don't want to live in Halls, where can I find Private Accomodation?

You can either book a Private accomodation via the Student Portal if you have got your Student Portal Username & Password at StudentPad. Alternatively, you can check rented rooms at SpareRoom.

Where can I find part time Jobs?

University of Greenwich Offers Part-Time role to students during thier time at Greenwich. You can see the current Student Vacancy at Student Jobshop. If you are looking for Part-time jobs in London, you can search at Indeed.

When Does the International Welcome Week starts?

You can get more details on the wesbite about International Welcome Week programe for International Students.

What about the Student Life at University of Greenwich?

Student Life at Greenwich is pretty fun as we are located at the heart of London giving our students an excellent opportunity to explore a city full of different culture. Student Life at Greenwich is fun as well as there are many activities and society to join during your time at Greenwich. You can also have a look at Greenwich's Student Website to know a bit more about societies and events.
